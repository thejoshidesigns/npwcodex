Mission

Helping people navigate unexpected change with God-centered hope.

Encouragement, faith-based guidance, meaningful conversations, and a supportive community centered on God.

Who we serve

Initial audience: State Farm employees and insurance professionals experiencing organizational change. Future audience: anyone facing career change, divorce, grief, health uncertainty, financial hardship, family challenge, AI-related displacement, retirement, or other unexpected events.

What we do

Listen, encourage, pray, share Scripture, publish resources, and connect people with hope.

What we do not do

No therapy, medical care, psychiatric treatment, legal advice, financial advice, or emergency support.

How we grow

Trust first, organic reach, moderated testimonies, and carefully protected community features.

Important: New Pathwalker offers faith-based encouragement, prayer, and supportive conversation. It is not medical advice, therapy, psychiatric treatment, legal advice, financial advice, or emergency support. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a crisis, contact local emergency services or a qualified crisis resource right away.
